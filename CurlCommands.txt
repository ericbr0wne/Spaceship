RootGet (get users table)
curl -X GET http://localhost:3000/get/users

RootPost (post user into users table)
curl -d "user=eric" -X POST http://localhost:3000/post/user

PostGamePlayers(post player1 and player2 into game table)
curl -d "user1_id=1&user2_id=2" -X POST http://localhost:3000/post/gameplayers
